About Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station in the state of Himachal Pradesh, located in the beautiful kullu valley along the banks of the beas river. Situated at the altitude of about 2,050 meters (6,726 feet). Tourists love visiting Hadimba Devi Temple, Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, Vashisht Temple, and Old Manali for their breathtaking beauty. You can enjoy paragliding and river rafting for thrilling experiences. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
